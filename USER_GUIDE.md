# 🌌 FiNet Intelligence Suite: User Guide

Welcome to **FiNet Intelligence Suite**, the next-generation financial operating system designed for CFOs, Finance Managers, and Audit Teams. This guide will walk you through the core functionalities and the powerful cross-module synchronization that powers your real-time financial truth.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started

### **The Dashboard**
Upon login, you are greeted by the **Financial Dashboard**. This is your real-time command center.
*   **KPI Cards**: Track your **Cash Position**, **Total Revenue**, and **Burn Rate** in real-time. These metrics update automatically as transactions are posted.
*   **Cash Flow Chart**: A 12-month visual breakdown of Inflows vs. Outflows.
*   **Recent Transactions**: A live feed of the latest financial activities across all departments.
*   **AI Strategic Insights**: Real-time advice generated by our AI core, highlighting revenue opportunities or cost-saving measures.

---

## 🧾 2. Expense Management

The **Expense Management** module is where team members submit spending reports and managers authorize them.

### **Submitting a Report**
1.  Navigate to **Expense Mgmt** in the sidebar.
2.  Click the **+ Submit New Report** button.
3.  Enter the **Description**, **Amount**, and **Category**.
4.  Click **Submit**. The report will appear as **PENDING** in the list.

### **Approval Workflow**
1.  Finance Managers can click **View Details** on any pending report.
2.  Review the submission details.
3.  Click **Approve Report**. 
4.  **Automatic Sync**: Once approved, the report status changes to **APPROVED**, and a corresponding entry is **automatically generated** in the Transaction Ledger.

---

## 🛡️ 3. Risk & Audit Discovery

FiNet uses AI to scan your transactions for anomalies, duplicate payments, and suspicious activities.

### **Reviewing Anomalies**
1.  Navigate to **Risk & Audit** in the sidebar.
2.  High-risk items are flagged in the **Anomaly Feed**.
3.  Hover over an anomaly to see the **Review & Post →** button.

### **Clearing Risks (Review & Post)**
1.  Click **Review & Post** to open the audit confirmation modal.
2.  If the transaction is verified, click **Post Audit Entry**.
3.  **Automatic Sync**: The anomaly flag is **cleared** in the Transaction Ledger, and the transaction status is updated to **"Posted"**.
4.  The Dashboard counter for "Open Anomalies" will decrement immediately.

### **Exporting Audit Logs**
Click the **Export Audit Log** button to generate a professional PDF report of all discovered and resolved risks for your compliance records.

---

## 📈 4. Transaction Ledger

The **Transaction Ledger** is the "Source of Truth" for your organization.

*   **Real-Time Feed**: Displays all income and expenses, including those automatically synchronized from the Expense and Risk modules.
*   **Anomaly Flags**: Look for the **⚠️ Red Warning Icons** in the "Audit Score" column. These indicate transactions that require attention in the Risk & Audit module.
*   **Spend Heatmap**: At the bottom of the page, view your total spend distribution across categories (e.g., Salaries, Software, Marketing).

---

## 👥 5. Team & User Management

### **Inviting Members**
1.  Navigate to **User Management**.
2.  Enter the **Email Address** and select a **Role** (Admin, Finance Manager, Auditor).
3.  Click **Send Invitation**.
4.  The user will receive an automated email via our Mailtrap-integrated service to join the suite.

## 🧠 6. Executive Insights

For a high-level strategic overview, visit the **Executive Insights** page.
*   **Financial Health Check**: Our AI analyzes your ledger to provide a "Health Score."
*   **Forecasts**: View projections for the next quarter based on current burn rates and revenue trends.

## 🔄 7. The "Closed-Loop" Sync Logic

FiNet is designed so that you never have to enter data twice.

| Action | Resulting Sync |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Approve Expense** | ➡️ Automatically adds a new entry to the **Ledger**. |
| **Post Risk Audit** | ➡️ Automatically clears flags on the matching **Ledger** entry. |
| **New Transaction** | ➡️ Automatically updates **Dashboard KPIs** and **Charts**. |
