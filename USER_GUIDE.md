## 🏛️ 1. Core Intelligence

### **Executive Dashboard**
The central hub for real-time visibility.
*   **Live KPIs**: Cash Position, Total Revenue, Burn Rate, and Days Outstanding.
*   **AI Strategic Insights**: Real-time push notifications regarding revenue acceleration or cost-saving opportunities.
*   **Cash Flow Matrix**: Interactive visualization of liquidity trends.

### **Executive Insights (Pro)**
*   **Financial Health Check**: A 0-100 score based on liquidity, debt-to-equity, and burn efficiency.
*   **Automated Forecasting**: Predictive modeling of cash runways.

### **Financial Reports**
*   **Income Statements**: Dynamic generation of P&L reports.
*   **Export Engine**: One-click export to **PDF (Professional Audit Grade)** or **Excel (Data Analysis Grade)**.

### **Transaction Ledger**
The immutable source of truth.
*   **GL Mapping**: Every transaction is mapped to a General Ledger code (e.g., 4000 for Revenue, 6600 for Salaries).
*   **Audit Scores**: Integrated risk markers (⚠️) appearing directly in the ledger rows.

## 📥 2. Data Acquisition

### **Data Extraction (AI OCR)**
*   **Upload Portal**: Drag-and-drop invoices, receipts, or bank statements.
*   **Intelligent Parsing**: AI automatically extracts Vendor, Date, Amount, and Tax IDs.
*   **Verification**: Review extracted data before it enters the "Autonomous Bookkeeping" pipeline.

## 🛡️ 3. Cash & Control

### **Autonomous Bookkeeping**
*   **No-Code Rules**: Set up logic to auto-categorize recurring payments.
*   **Real-time Ledger Posting**: Once verified, data flows instantly into the global transactions.

### **AI Reconciliation (Pro)**
*   **Matching Engine**: Automatically matches bank statements against internal invoices/bills.
*   **Exception Handling**: AI flags discrepancies (e.g., partial payments or bank fees) for manual review.

### **Risk & Audit Discovery (Pro)**

## 📅 4. Planning & Compliance

### **Budgeting & Burn Control**
*   **Departmental Budgets**: Set limits for Marketing, R&D, and Ops.
*   **Over-spend Alerts**: Instant notifications when actual spend exceeds 80% of budget.

### **Scenario Planning (Pro)**
*   **What-If Analysis**: Simulate the impact of a 20% headcount increase or a 10% revenue drop.
*   **Runway Extrapolation**: Visualizes how different decisions affect the company's "Zero Cash Date."

### **ESG Reporting**
*   **Carbon Footprint Tracking**: Calculates emissions based on travel and utility expenses.
*   **Social Impact Score**: Metrics on vendor diversity and governance compliance.

### **Tax Center & Payroll**
*   **Tax Liability Estimation**: Real-time calculation of corporate tax obligations.
*   **Payroll Sync**: Integrated view of salary disbursements, bonuses, and benefits.

## 🌍 5. Market & Strategic Intelligence

### **Market Intelligence (Pro)**
*   **Competitor Benchmarking**: Compare your burn rate and growth metrics against industry averages.

## ⚙️ 6. Configuration

### **Integrations**
*   **Bank Connect**: Link accounts via Plaid or direct API.
*   **ERP Sync**: Connect to SAP, Oracle, or NetSuite.

### **User Management**
*   **RBAC (Role Based Access Control)**:
    *   **Admin**: Full access.
    *   **Auditor**: View-only + Risk posting.
    *   **Team Member**: Expense submission only.

### **Settings**
*   **Theme Engine**: Switch between Sky, Rose, Emerald, and Violet aesthetics.
*   **Dark Mode**: Optimized for low-light financial auditing.
